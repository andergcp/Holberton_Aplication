Redefining Subjects


Nowadays we are teaching in the most of our schools subjects from the past, a vast majority of these subjects won't be useful for our children in the future.

That's why is very important to re-think and re-define the skills, knowledge, information and capabilities we want to transmit to the coming generations.

Subjects for real life

As we said above, its very important to teach to our children what they need to be really happy and successful for all their lives.

That's why there is a mandatory component and an optional one:

Mandatory subjects

There are some necessary subjects we have to learn to be successful and happy, all our children have to learn some basic skills and knowledge, these are which that we propose:

  • Mathematics
  • Environment
  • Computing Science



  • Emotional Intelligence
  • Personal Finance
  • Soft Skills



  • Sports and Healt
  • Art and Culture
  • History and Law

Optional subjects

We understand that every child is different, all of us have different hobbies, interests and passions. That's why we offer a lot of options to explore and go in depth according to the child's passions and interests, from science to arts, from tech to sports.
