Many institutions from diferent countries

Little and big institutions and organizations around the world are exploring different ways to grow up our children:

The Montessori Method is a great example of this.

The Montessori Method is characterized by providing a prepared environment: tidy, pleasing in appearance, simple and real, where each element exists for a reason in order to help in the development of the child. A Montessori classroom integrates children of mixed ages that are grouped in periods of 3 years. This promotes socialization, respect and solidarity among them naturally.(Source:www.fundacionmontessori.org)
