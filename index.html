Education for Life


Edu4Life is looking for a good education system that really help our children out to be happy and successful people. Citizens who build a better world while are working and expending their time doing what the love and what they are good at.

Why a diferent education?

For decades we've been spending our childhood "learning" a lot of items, subjects, consuming a lot of information given from the teacher as a super human, the owner of the knowledge. But we forget it some years, months o days later because we never use it nor really learn it.

That's why it's so important to spend our time learning what we'll really use and will help us to be a better human being.

Is the current education system the right way?

Empirically we've seen and lived that the most of the content we receive in the school is not useful in the real life. Sometimes, we feel that all the time we spent in the school was lost.

Even though there are not enough scientific studies on the topic, a vast majority of people who have been in the traditional education school don't feel confortable and happy with what they learnt during school.
